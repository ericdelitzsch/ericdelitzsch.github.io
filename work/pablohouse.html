Pablo House

The project was born from the need to promote and develop the reconstruction of the town of Jojutla, Morelos, which was seriously affected during the earthquake on September 19, 2017. The approach is to generate a regenerative impact in the local community, since the construction methods used are very economical, anti-seismic, ecologically sustainable and quick to build. Starting from integrating the community in the design and construction process, knowledge is shared in order to empower people in their current circumstances.
