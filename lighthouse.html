Light House

Once an essential water navigation tool, this design successfully preserves the conceptual elements of the lighthouse building type.
The design is based on a torch, where the lighting generated by the tower depends on the people who inhabit it, generating different tones and intensities, assimilating to a torch. The building has 3 floors, where the social area, a living room, kitchen, dining room and terrace, are on the first floor. On the second floor there is a bedroom with a bathroom and on the third floor, there is a contemplation area, where at night, it becomes the source of the lighthouse.
